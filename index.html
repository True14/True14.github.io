About Me

Hi, I'm Jonathan Fitzgibbon, a junior level developer from Columbia, South Carolina. I graduated from the University of South Carolina in 2015 with a B.S. in Computer Science.

I cut my teeth on project workflow through Thinkful's fulltime web development course where I grew fond of working with front-end technologies like React and Redux. I also have a new found fondness for Test Driven Development.

I do prefer working with the front-end, but in general I just love solving problems. It doesn't matter where the problem resides (front-end, back-end), I love investigating the problem and figuring out how things work.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy riding my bike, playing video games, and reading books.
