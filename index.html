About Me

Hi, I'm Jonathan Fitzgibbon, a developer from Columbia, South Carolina. I graduated from the University of South Carolina in 2015 with a B.S. in Computer Science.

I'm fond of building projects with front-end technologies like React and Redux. I also love Test Driven Development.

I do prefer working with the front-end, but in general I'm really passionate about solving problems especially if the answer is not clear right away. It doesn't matter where the problem resides (front-end, back-end), I'm fascinated by investigating the problem and figuring out how things work.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy riding my bike, playing video games, and reading books. The games I currently play the most are Final Fantasy 14 and Mario Kart 8. My favorite author is Patrick Rothfuss.
